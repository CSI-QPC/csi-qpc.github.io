# Expertise

Our expertise spans quantitative proteomics approaches for studying protein abundance, interactions, and spatial organization. We support a broad range of biological questions across discovery, hypothesis-driven, and translational studies.

**Proteome analysis**

We support global and targeted proteome analyses to quantify protein expression across biological conditions. This includes comparative studies, perturbation experiments, and analysis of complex samples such as tissues and clinical materials.

**Imaging mass spectrometry**

We support imaging mass spectrometry approaches for spatially resolved proteomics. These workflows enable the investigation of protein distribution and localization within tissues, complementing bulk proteomics measurements with spatial context.

**Study types and applications**

Our expertise supports a range of study types, including:
- Discovery and comparative proteomics
- Mechanistic studies and pathway analysis
- Clinical and translational research
- Tissue-based and spatial proteomics applications
